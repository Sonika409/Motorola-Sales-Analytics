# Motorola-Sales-Analytics

## Motorola Sales Analysis Dashboard
An interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze mobile sales performance, customer behavior, and payment trends.

The dashboard provides insights into total sales, transactions, product performance, and regional demand, enabling businesses to understand customer purchasing patterns and improve decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 Microsoft Power BI – Used to design interactive dashboards and visualizations
* 📂 Microsoft Excel – Used as the primary data source and for initial data exploration
* 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and business logic
* 🔄 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and loading into Power BI

## 📂 Data Source
The dataset used in this dashboard contains mobile sales data including:
* Sales transactions across multiple cities
* Mobile models and brands
* Quantity sold
* Payment methods used by customers
* Customer ratings
* Time-based data (months and days)

## 🚀 Features / Highlights
### 🔹 Business Problem
Retail businesses often collect large volumes of sales data, but extracting meaningful insights from spreadsheets is difficult.
* Decision-makers need visibility into:
* Which cities generate the highest sales
* Which mobile models perform best
*,Which payment methods customers prefer
* Which months or days generate the highest sales
* Without proper visualization, identifying these trends becomes time-consuming.

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive analytics dashboard that:
* Tracks overall sales performance
* Identifies top-selling mobile models
* Analyzes regional sales distribution
* Understands customer behavior and payment preferences
* Helps businesses make data-driven sales strategies

### 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 🔢 KPI Cards (Top Section)
Key metrics displayed:
* Total Sales: 769M
* Average Sales: 40K
* Total Transactions: 4K
* Total Quantity Sold: 19K

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

#### 🗺️ Total Sales by City (Map Visualization)
A geographic visualization showing sales distribution across Indian cities.
Insights:
* Identifies high-revenue cities
* Helps companies optimize regional sales strategies
* Reveals geographic demand patterns

#### 📈 Total Quantity by Month (Line Chart)
Displays monthly sales quantity trends.
Helps identify:
* Peak demand months
* Seasonal buying patterns
* Sales fluctuations over time

#### ⭐ Customer Ratings (Funnel Chart)
Shows the distribution of customer ratings from 1 to 5.
* Insights include:
* Overall customer satisfaction
* Areas where product/service quality may need improvement

#### 💳 Transactions by Payment Method (Pie Chart)
Breakdown of customer payment preferences:
* UPI
* Debit Card
* Cash
* Credit Card

This helps businesses understand how customers prefer to pay.

#### 📱 Total Sales by Mobile Model (Bar Chart)
Displays top-performing smartphone models such as:
* iPhone SE
* OnePlus models
* Samsung Galaxy models

Helps identify best-selling devices and product demand.

#### 📅 Total Sales by Day Name (Area Chart)
Analyzes sales distribution across days of the week.
Useful for:
* Identifying high-traffic sales days
* Planning promotions and marketing campaigns

#### 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Users can dynamically explore data using filters such as:
* Month filter
* Mobile model filter
* Brand filter
* Payment method filter
* Day filter

These slicers allow customized analysis for deeper insights.

#### 📈 Business Impact & Insights

* 📍 Regional Sales Strategy :
The city map highlights high-performing markets, helping businesses focus on strong regions.

* 📊 Product Demand Analysis :
Identifies the most popular smartphone models among customers.

* 💳 Customer Payment Behavior :
Shows strong adoption of digital payments like UPI and cards.

* 📅 Sales Timing Insights :
Weekly and monthly trends help businesses optimize inventory and marketing campaigns.
